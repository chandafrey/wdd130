Beginner

Take a day trip down the river and enjoy the beauty of the Goodwin Canyon River. This trip offers an orientation, 2 hours of rafting followed by a lunch break, then another 3 hours of rafting and finally a BBQ dinner to finish off the day.

Schedule Beginner Trips

Intermediate

Take a 3 day adventure trip enjoying the wonders of North Fork American river or Kaweah River. This trip begins with orientation, 2-3 hours of rafting, lunch and rest, 2-3 hours of rafting and then enjoy the rest of the evening with dinner and camping. Day 2 and 3 will begin with breakfast before heading out.

Schedule Intermediate Trips

Advanced

Take a 5 day adventure trip and enjoy the thrill of either Merced River or Cherry Creek River. This trip begins with orientation, 2-3 hours of rafting, lunch and rest, 2-3 hours of rafting and then enjoy the rest of the evening with dinner and camping. Days 2-5 will begin with breakfast before heading out for the days adventures.

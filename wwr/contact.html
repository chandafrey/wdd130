DRY OAR Whitewater Rafting

DRY OAR Whitewater Rafting offers rafting trips for beginner, intermediate, and advanced rafters.

Group rafting trips are a great way to build confidence and team comradery - whether you are a family, friends or corporate group.

Rafting trips are also a great 'bonus' option to offer employees especially if you offer 'plus one' opportunities.

History

DRY OAR Whitewater Rafting has been serving adventurists since 1990. We started out as a few friends rafting together every summer. Over time, we would host trips for family reunions and co-workers. We discovered we had even more enjoyment in helping others discover the thrill of rafting and thus DRY OAR/ Whitewater Rafting was born.

DRY OAR Whitewater Rafting has spent the last 34 years exploring and navigating the many rivers here in California. We have categorized which rivers are suitable for beginner, intermediate and advanced adventurists. We have developed trip packages with focus on taking the work out of it for you. Come enjoy a rafting experience with confidence in your rafting guide and preplanned rest stops for meals, nature, and camping. (3 and 5 day trip packages include camping.)
